The Grid

The bootstrap grid system has classes

  • xs (extra small, for screens less than 768px)
  • sm (small, for screens 768px and up)
  • md (medium, for screens 992px and up)
  • lg (large, for screens 1200px and up)
  • xl (extra large, for screens 1400px and up)

Adjusts according to screen.
